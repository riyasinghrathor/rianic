About

The person behind the cuts.

I started editing in 2018 — cutting gaming montages in my bedroom with cracked software and a dream. Six months later, my first paying client sent me $500 for a YouTube video that blew up to 2M views.

That moment changed everything. I realized editing isn't just about making things look good — it's about understanding human attention, emotional pacing, and the psychology of why people click, watch, and convert.

Today, I work with creators and brands across 12 countries. I've edited over 200 projects, from 15-second Reels to 60-minute documentaries. Every project gets the same obsessive attention to detail.
